# Routing Traffic

With the proper setup, TunnelBox will automatically route traffic to different 
ports on your network. The step for setup are listed below.

## Step 1

After registering a domain, navigate to the Domain Page by selecting
the Domain tab on the left side of the page.

Select the Domain you would like to add routing too.

## Step 2 

Select the add route button and fill out the relevant fields. Click "Save Route" to save.
