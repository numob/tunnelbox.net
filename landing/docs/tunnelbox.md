# TunnelBox Registration

## Step 1 : Power & Ethernet
First, connect your tunnel box to a power source. Next, connect your box
to the internet via ethernet.


## Step 2 : Registering Your Box
#### Step 2a : Acount Creation
Login in to https://console.tunnelbox.net and create an account.


#### Step 2b : Setup Wizard
Click "Activate TunnelBox" on the dashboard.
