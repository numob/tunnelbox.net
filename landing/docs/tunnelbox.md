# TunnelBox Registration

This guide helps first-time users activate a TunnelBox device.

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:

- A TunnelBox device with power adapter
- Ethernet access to your router/network
- A browser and internet connection
- An account at [console.tunnelbox.net](https://console.tunnelbox.net)

## Step 1: Connect Hardware

1. Connect the TunnelBox to power.
2. Connect Ethernet from TunnelBox to your router or switch.
3. Wait for the device to boot.

## Step 2: Sign In to the Console

1. Open [console.tunnelbox.net](https://console.tunnelbox.net).
2. Create an account or sign in.

## Step 3: Activate the Device

1. Open the dashboard.
2. Click `Activate TunnelBox`.
3. Enter the device details requested by the wizard.
4. Confirm activation.

## Step 4: Verify Online Status

After activation, check that the device appears as:

- `Online` or `Connected`
- Ready for domain and route configuration

## Common First-Run Checks

- If the device is offline, confirm Ethernet is connected.
- If activation fails, refresh and retry after 1-2 minutes.
- If still blocked, use the [Troubleshooting](/docs/troubleshooting) guide.
