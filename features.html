Expose local services. Securely. Instantly.

Tunnelbox is a hardware tunneling device that lets developers expose local apps and services to the internet without network configuration or TLS headaches.

Built for developers

Tunnelbox creates a hardware gateway from your local machine or on-premise environment to the public internet.

No CLI daemons. No background agents. No brittle configs. Just hardware that does one job extremely well.

  • ✔ No Additional inbound ports
  • ✔ Quick and Easy Setup
  • ✔ Cloud-managed DNS
  • ✔ Designed for local-first workflows

Core features

No network configuration

Tunnelbox works out of the box. Connect your box to a router and your set.

Plug it into any network and it connects securely using outbound-only traffic. No NAT. No cable mess.

Automatic SSL / TLS

Every exposed service is protected with HTTPS by default. Certificates are provisioned and renewed automatically.

No certbot. No manual renewals. No broken demos.

True plug & play

Power it on, claim it, and you’re live. Tunnelbox self-registers and provisions itself without local setup or configuration files.

Free managed domains

Instantly expose services using built-in managed domains. Route multiple domains to different local ports and services on the same Tunnelbox device.

  • One domain per service
  • HTTPS included
  • Public or restricted access

Made for real dev workflows

Perfect for live demos, webhooks, dashboards, internal tools, staging environments, and customer previews.

Tunnelbox keeps your local setup intact while making it reachable from anywhere.
